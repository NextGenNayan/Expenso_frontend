Frequently asked

Quick answers before you write in

Is my data uploaded anywhere?

No. Expenso stores every transaction in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to a server.

Can I use Expenso on my phone?

Yes — the whole site is fully responsive, from the dashboard cards down to the transaction list.

Can I export my transactions?

Open the Reports page and use "Export CSV" to download the transactions in your selected date range.

Will I lose my data if I clear my browser?

Clearing your browser's site data will reset Expenso back to its sample transactions, so export first if you want to keep a copy.
